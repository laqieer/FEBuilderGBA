Honest scope: the source body is the LZ77-DECOMPRESSED 4bpp payload; --verify-asset
re-decompresses the live ROM block and READ-ONLY byte-compares. This is source-level
structure-exact identity + read-only byte-exact ROM compare, NOT a byte-pinned ROM
re-insertion (FEBuilder's LZ77 packer is non-canonical → the decomp build re-compresses).
srcAddr is provenance metadata ONLY. FE7 obj2 high-byte split tileset is a separate
stream/--addr (out of scope). READ-ONLY: never mutates CoreState.ROM.
